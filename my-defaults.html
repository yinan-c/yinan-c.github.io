My App Defaults

Inspired by Hemispheric Views 097 - Duel of the Defaults! and blog posts by many. I would like to write my own defaults too.

  • 📮 Mail Server: iCloud and Gmail

  • 📨 Mail Client: iOS: Spark and Mail.app and macOS: Canary Mail and Mail.app

  • 📝 Notes: Obsidian and DEVONthink

  • ✅ To-Do: Previously Things, now OmniFocus

  • 📷 iPhone Photo Shooting: Camera.app

  • 🟦 Photos Management: Photos.app

  • 📆 Calendar: Calendar.app (Personal) and Outlook (Work)

  • 📅 Calendar Backend: iCloud (Personal) and Nexus365 (Work)

  • 📁 Cloud File Storage: iCloud Drive (Personal) and Google Drive (Work)

  • 📖 RSS: Previously Reeder with FreshRSS, Now NetNewsWire iCloud account

  • 🙍🏻‍♂️ Contacts Management: Contacts.app

  • 🌐 Browser: Safari on iOS, and Arc on my Mac

  • 💬 Chat: WeChat, Telegram, Slack and Discord

  • 🔖 Bookmarks: Anybox and DEVONthink

  • 📑 Read-it-Later: OmniFocus and DEVONthink

  • 📜 Word Processing: Google Docs

  • 📈 Spreadsheets: Google Sheets

  • 📊 Presentations: Keynote.app, and PDF generated from Markdown using Pandoc or LaTeX.

  • 🛒 Shopping List: OmniFocus

  • 🍴 Meal Planning (as Recipe Management): N/A, not that I don't eat/cook, but I don't have a meal planning system.

  • 💰 Budgeting and finance: Cookie

  • 📰 News: From podcast, RSS feeds and Twitter.

  • 🎵 Music: Apple Music, sometimes on Bilibili

  • 🎤 Podcast: 小宇宙 for Chinese channels and Apple Podcasts for all others. I am also using Podwise.xyz as an AI tool.

  • 🔐 Password Management: 1Password

  • 🧑‍💻 Code Editor: Visual Studio Code
